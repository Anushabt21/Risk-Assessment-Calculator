Risk Assessment Calculator

Assess Likelihood

Threat Actor Factors

Skills The technical expertise the threat actor requires to identify and exploit the risk.





Motive The motive for threat actor to exploit the risk based on the level on reward/benefit.





Privileges Required The degree of accessibility threat actor requires to exploit the risk.





Attack Vector The method and/or location threat actor can exploit the risky component.





Vulnerability Factors

Ease of Discovery Vulnerability is present in the component, likelihood that threat actor identifies such a vulnerability.





Ease of Exploit The level of effort threat actor requires to exploit the vulnerability, can be combined with expertise required.






Exploit Awareness Knowledge and understanding about the vulnerability and the exploit methods availability.





Attack Detection The ease and possibility with which the intrusion can be identified, if the threat actor successfully exploits the vulnerability.





Assess Impact

Technical Impact

Loss of Confidentiality The extent to which the confidential sensitive and critical information can be exposed that can arise from the exploit of the risk.





Loss of Integrity The extent to which accuracy and consistency of the data can be affected that can arise from the exploit of the risk.





Loss of Availability The extent to which the accessibility of data and system can be affected that can arise from the exploit of the risk.





Loss of Accountability The extent to which the activities can be traced and recognise the responsible entity that can arise from the exploit of the risk.





Business Impact

Operational Impact The potential disruption to organizational processes and workflows resulting from the exploitation of the risk.





Internal Non-Compliance The potential for violations of internally agreed baselines, policies, standards, procedures and guidelines that may arise from the risk or from exploitation of the risk.





Financial Damage The level of economic impact on the organization that can arise from the exploit of the risk.





Reputation Damage The potential for negative impact on the trust and credibility of the product and organization that can arise from the exploit of the risk.
